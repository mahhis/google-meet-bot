Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 06, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of your information when You use the Service.

Information We Collect

To provide the service, our Telegram bot collects and stores the following information:

  • Your Telegram User ID, to associate your Google Account with your Telegram account.
  • Your Google Refresh Token (stored encrypted), which is required to create Google Meet links on your behalf without asking for your password repeatedly.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Generate Google Meet links as requested by you.
  • Create calendar events in your Google Calendar for the generated meetings.

We do not read any other data from your Google Account. We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties.
